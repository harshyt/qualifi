# Screener Design System

## Brand
AI-powered resume screening platform for technical recruiters. Tone: calm, trustworthy, professional, decision-support.

## Colors
- Primary: Soft Azure #2196F3
- Background: Cloud White #F9FAFB
- Surface: #FFFFFF with subtle 1px #E2E8F0 borders
- Text Primary: #1A1A2E
- Text Secondary: #64748B
- Verdict SHORTLIST: #4CAF50 (green chip)
- Verdict PENDING: #FF9800 (amber chip)
- Verdict REJECT: #F44336 (red chip)

## Style
- Material 3 light & airy aesthetic
- No heavy drop shadows
- Generous whitespace
- Skeleton loaders for async states
- Scannable data-dense layouts
